This is going to be a long one! Is that an excuse for how long it took me to post? No. No it is not. That being said, I am accountable to nobody but myself, and I'm apparently an extremely lenient manager. Anyway, this grew out of a habit I got into of taking pictures out my window with the eventual hope of stitching them together into a lovely seasonal troupe. I did not do that, because I'm lazy. Thanks to the magic of modern technology I can revisit that failure! Did it take me longer to put all this together than it would have to just keep a little photo schedule? Oh, most definitely. Long story short, I rigged a dusty raspberry pi to an even dustier DSLR and python-ed it into taking a picture every ten minutes around sunset. It even uploads the resulting tidbits to the clouuuuud. How fun!
