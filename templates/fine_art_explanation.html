The Met Art API

A quick look at the Met's art api. I mention async python. I don't go into detail, because I don't know what I'm doing.

I can't really call myself an art person. I love art, but I'm not exactly an educated art consumer. I love landscapes, though, and I hate hosting my own images. I remember hearing something about the Met supporting an api for its collection and thought "gosh, wouldn't that be neat."

Said API is documented here, and it's pretty full featured. You basically get a bunch of objectID integers that correspond to a query (I haven't played around with their query engine too much, although it seems...interestingly structured; hit refresh a few times and you'll notice the variety of images that correspond to the query keyword "landscape").
